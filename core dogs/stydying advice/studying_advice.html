Studying Advice

Much of the advice people give about studying is wrong.
Always study in the same place?

Wrong!!

Study one topic intensively before going to the next?

Wrong!!

Here are some study tips that are based on actual research.

From Forgot What You Know About Good Study Habits, by Benedict carey, published in the NewYork Times on September 6,2010

Alternative study environments

Study in the library, in the coffee shop, in your room, ... Varying the place you study helps you remember more.

Mix content

Study a bit of this, a bit of that, some this again, some of the other. Switching content helps you remember more.

Space study sessions

Three one-hour sessions is more efficient than one three-hour session.

Self-testing

Test yourself, or have a buddy do it. It helps you kow how well you know.CoreDogs is good at this. That's what the review system is.
